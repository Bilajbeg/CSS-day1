Rising poverty in Austria

The Authors

  • Peter Vienna
  • Micheal Helsinki
  • Sandra Paris

Short summary

A phenomenon after the enormous price increases is the increasingly
visible impoverishment of our population. Above all, the poor are
getting poorer and the middle class is sinking to the level of yesterday's poor.
What can be done to stop this phenomenon?
